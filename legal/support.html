Frequently Asked Questions

How do I backup my data?
Pearl offers optional iCloud backup. To enable it, go to your device's Settings > [Your Name] > iCloud > Manage Storage > Pearl, and toggle on iCloud backup. You can also export your data manually through the app's export feature.
Can I use Pearl on multiple devices?
Yes! Pearl supports cross-device synchronization through iCloud. Simply sign in with the same Apple ID on all your devices and enable iCloud backup for Pearl. Your data will sync automatically across iPhone and iPad.
Is my medical data secure?
Absolutely. Pearl is designed with privacy-first principles. All your data is stored locally on your device by default. iCloud backup is optional and uses Apple's secure encryption. We never access or share your medical content.
How do I export my data?
You can export your data in multiple formats through the app's settings. Go to Settings > Export Data to save your pearls as text files, which you can then transfer to your computer or other devices.
What if I lose my device?
If you have iCloud backup enabled, you can restore your data on a new device by signing in with the same Apple ID and restoring from iCloud. If you don't have backup enabled, we recommend regularly exporting your data as a safety measure.
Can I get a refund?
Refunds are processed through the Apple App Store. You can request a refund through your App Store account or by contacting Apple Support directly. We cannot process refunds ourselves.
