Frequently Asked Questions

How do I backup my data?

Pearl offers iCloud backup. Go to Settings ‚Üí iCloud Backup ‚Üí Enable. You can also export your data manually through the app's export feature.

Can I use Pearl on multiple devices?

Yes! With iCloud backup enabled, your pearls will sync across all your iOS devices signed into the same Apple ID.

How do I export my medical notes?

Go to Settings ‚Üí Export Data. You can export as text files, which you can then save to Files, email, or share with other apps.

Is my medical data secure?

Yes! All data is stored locally on your device by default. iCloud backup is encrypted and optional. We never access your medical content.

How do I organize my pearls by specialty?

Use the tagging feature when creating pearls. You can add multiple tags like "Cardiology", "Emergency Medicine", etc., and filter by tags later.

Can I edit audio transcriptions?

Yes! After recording, you can edit the transcribed text before saving your audio pearl.

What if I lose my device?

If you have iCloud backup enabled, you can restore your pearls on a new device. Always keep regular backups of important medical information.

How do I search through my pearls?

Use the search bar at the top of the main screen. You can search by text content, tags, or pearl type (text, audio, photo).

Technical Support

Before Contacting Support

  • Check that your iOS version is 15.0 or later
  • Ensure you have sufficient storage space
  • Try restarting the app
  • Check your internet connection for iCloud features
  • Update to the latest version of Pearl

When Contacting Support

Please include the following information:

  • Your iOS version
  • Pearl app version
  • Device model
  • Detailed description of the issue
  • Screenshots if applicable
  • Steps to reproduce the problem

Feature Requests

We love hearing from users about new features! Send your suggestions to support@pearlapp.com with "Feature Request" in the subject line.

Bug Reports

If you encounter a bug, please report it to support@pearlapp.com with "Bug Report" in the subject line. Include as much detail as possible to help us fix it quickly.
