Frequently Asked Questions

How do I backup my medical data?
Pearl stores all your data locally on your device. To backup your data, enable iCloud backup in your device settings. Your medical content will be automatically backed up to your iCloud account with end-to-end encryption.
Can I export my medical notes?
Yes! Pearl allows you to export your medical content in various formats including PDF, text, and JSON. Use the export feature in the app settings to download your data.
Is my medical data secure?
Absolutely. Pearl is designed with medical data security in mind. All data is stored locally on your device and never transmitted to our servers unless you explicitly enable iCloud backup.
What devices are supported?
Pearl is available for iOS devices running iOS 15.0 or later. This includes iPhone and iPad models. We're working on expanding to other platforms.
How do I sync across multiple devices?
Enable iCloud backup in your device settings to automatically sync your Pearl data across all your iOS devices signed in with the same Apple ID.
Can I use Pearl offline?
Yes! Pearl works completely offline. All core features including note creation, audio recording, and photo capture work without an internet connection.
