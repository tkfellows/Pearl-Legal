Executive Summary

Pearl is a medical knowledge management application designed for healthcare professionals to capture, organize, and retrieve clinical pearls, case notes, and medical insights. The app is designed with privacy-first principles and complies with medical app regulations and standards.

Key Compliance Highlights

  • HIPAA Compliant: Local storage, optional encrypted iCloud backup
  • Medical Disclaimers: Clear disclaimers throughout the app
  • Privacy-First: No external data transmission, user-controlled data
  • Professional Use: Designed for medical education and knowledge management
