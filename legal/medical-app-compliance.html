Professional Use Guidelines

Pearl is designed for use by healthcare professionals and medical students for educational and professional development purposes:

  • Target Users: Physicians, nurses, medical students, and healthcare professionals
  • Intended Use: Medical knowledge organization, clinical pearl documentation, and professional development
  • Professional Standards: Users should comply with their institutional policies and professional guidelines
  • Verification: All medical information should be verified against authoritative sources
  • Patient Privacy: Users should not store patient-identifiable information in the app

Compliance Monitoring & Updates

Pearl maintains ongoing compliance monitoring and updates:

  • Regular Reviews: Quarterly compliance assessments and updates
  • Regulatory Updates: Monitoring changes in medical app regulations
  • User Feedback: Incorporating professional user feedback for compliance improvements
  • Security Audits: Regular security assessments and penetration testing
  • Documentation Updates: Keeping compliance documentation current
