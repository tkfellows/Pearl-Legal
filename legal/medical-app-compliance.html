Medical App Guidelines Compliance

Pearl - Medical Knowledge Management App

FDA Compliant HIPAA Ready International Standards

Important Disclaimer

Pearl is a medical knowledge management tool, not a medical device or clinical decision support system. It is designed to help healthcare professionals organize their medical knowledge and clinical pearls. Users are responsible for their own clinical decisions and compliance with applicable medical regulations.

FDA Medical Device Regulations

Medical Device Classification

Not Applicable
Requirement: Medical devices must be classified and regulated by the FDA.
Implementation: Pearl is not a medical device. It is a knowledge management tool that does not diagnose, treat, or provide clinical decision support. Users create and organize their own medical knowledge.

Clinical Decision Support

Not Applicable
Requirement: Clinical decision support software must meet FDA requirements.
Implementation: Pearl does not provide clinical decision support. It is a personal knowledge management tool for organizing user-created content.

HIPAA Compliance

Protected Health Information

User Responsibility
Requirement: Apps handling PHI must comply with HIPAA regulations.
Implementation: Pearl stores data locally on the user's device. Users are responsible for ensuring any PHI they store complies with HIPAA. The app does not transmit data to external servers.

Data Security

Compliant
Requirement: Medical data must be securely stored and transmitted.
Implementation: Pearl uses iOS security features including device encryption, Face ID/Touch ID, and secure iCloud backup. All data remains on the user's device.

Business Associate Agreements

Not Applicable
Requirement: Apps that process PHI on behalf of covered entities must have BAAs.
Implementation: Pearl does not process PHI on behalf of any entity. Users manage their own data independently.
