App Store Review Guidelines Compliance

Pearl - Medical Knowledge Management App

Safety & Privacy Performance Ready Legal Compliant

Safety & Privacy

App Privacy

Compliant
Requirement: Apps must request permission to access user data and explain how the data will be used.
Implementation: Pearl requests specific permissions (camera, microphone, photo library, speech recognition) with clear explanations of medical use cases. All data is stored locally on the user's device.

Data Collection & Storage

Compliant
Requirement: Apps must have a privacy policy and handle user data appropriately.
Implementation: Pearl has a comprehensive privacy policy. All medical data is stored locally with optional iCloud backup. No data is transmitted to external servers.

Health & Medical Information

Compliant
Requirement: Apps that handle health information must comply with applicable laws and regulations.
Implementation: Pearl is designed for medical knowledge management, not patient care. Users are responsible for their own data and compliance with HIPAA and other regulations.

Performance & Functionality

App Completeness

Compliant
Requirement: Apps must be complete and functional when submitted.
Implementation: Pearl is fully functional with all advertised features working: text, audio, and photo pearl creation; tagging system; iCloud backup; search functionality.

Beta Testing

Compliant
Requirement: Apps should be thoroughly tested before submission.
Implementation: Pearl has been tested on multiple devices and iOS versions. All core functionality has been validated.

Accurate Metadata

Compliant
Requirement: App metadata must be accurate and not misleading.
Implementation: Pearl's description accurately reflects its functionality as a medical knowledge management tool for healthcare professionals.

Business & Monetization

Pricing

Compliant
Requirement: App pricing must be clear and consistent.
Implementation: Pearl is a one-time purchase ($4.99) with no in-app purchases, subscriptions, or hidden fees.

In-App Purchases

Not Applicable
Requirement: In-app purchases must use Apple's payment system.
Implementation: Pearl does not include any in-app purchases.

Design & User Experience

User Interface

Compliant
Requirement: Apps must follow Apple's Human Interface Guidelines.
Implementation: Pearl uses native SwiftUI components and follows Apple's design principles with proper spacing, typography, and accessibility features.

Accessibility

Compliant
Requirement: Apps should be accessible to users with disabilities.
Implementation: Pearl includes VoiceOver support, proper contrast ratios, and accessible navigation for users with disabilities.

App Store Screenshots

Compliant
Requirement: Screenshots must accurately represent the app's functionality.
Implementation: Screenshots will show actual app functionality without misleading elements or overlays.

Legal & Intellectual Property

Intellectual Property

Compliant
Requirement: Apps must not infringe on intellectual property rights.
Implementation: Pearl is an original application created by the developer. All code, design, and content are original or properly licensed.

Legal Compliance

Compliant
Requirement: Apps must comply with all applicable laws and regulations.
Implementation: Pearl complies with relevant privacy laws and medical app regulations. Users are responsible for their own compliance with medical practice laws.

Technical Requirements

App Binary

Compliant
Requirement: Apps must be properly signed and not contain malicious code.
Implementation: Pearl is properly signed with the developer's certificate and contains only legitimate, functional code.

API Usage

Compliant
Requirement: Apps must use APIs as intended and not interfere with system functionality.
Implementation: Pearl uses standard iOS APIs for camera, microphone, speech recognition, and iCloud services as intended.

Encryption

Compliant
Requirement: Apps using encryption must declare their usage.
Implementation: Pearl declares "ITSAppUsesNonExemptEncryption: false" as it only uses standard iOS encryption.

Content & Behavior

Objectionable Content

Compliant
Requirement: Apps must not contain objectionable content.
Implementation: Pearl is a professional medical app with no objectionable content. All content is medical and educational in nature.

Spam

Not Applicable
Requirement: Apps must not send spam or unwanted communications.
Implementation: Pearl does not send any communications or notifications to users.
