App Store Server Notifications

Last updated: January 2025

Important: This document provides information about App Store Server Notifications for Pearl purchases. These notifications help ensure secure and reliable transaction processing.

Overview

App Store Server Notifications are automated messages sent by Apple to our servers when events occur related to your Pearl purchase. These notifications help us maintain accurate records and provide better service to our users.

What Are App Store Server Notifications?

App Store Server Notifications are secure, automated messages that Apple sends to our servers when certain events happen with your Pearl purchase, such as:

  • Successful purchase completion
  • Purchase refunds or cancellations
  • Subscription status changes (if applicable in the future)
  • Billing issues or payment failures
  • Account changes affecting purchases

How It Works

1. Purchase Process

Initial Purchase

When you purchase Pearl for $4.99, Apple processes the payment and sends us a notification confirming the successful transaction.

2. Notification Types

CONSUMPTION_REQUEST

Sent when Apple needs to verify that Pearl has been properly delivered to your device. This helps prevent fraud and ensures you receive what you paid for.

REFUND

Sent when a refund is processed through the App Store. This helps us maintain accurate records of your purchase status.

REFUND_DECLINED

Sent when a refund request is declined by Apple, typically due to policy violations or timing restrictions.

Data Security and Privacy

  • Secure Transmission: All notifications are sent over encrypted HTTPS connections
  • Limited Data: Notifications contain only transaction-related information, not your personal medical data
  • No Medical Content: Your Pearl content and medical notes are never included in these notifications
  • Apple's Responsibility: Apple handles all payment processing and notification delivery
  • Our Use: We use this data only for transaction verification and customer support

What Information Is Included

App Store Server Notifications may include:

  • Transaction identifiers
  • Purchase dates and times
  • Product identifiers (Pearl app)
  • Purchase amounts
  • Refund information (if applicable)
  • Account identifiers (anonymized)
Important: App Store Server Notifications do not contain any of your medical content, personal notes, or clinical pearls. Your medical data remains completely separate from transaction processing.

Technical Implementation

Our servers are configured to receive and process these notifications securely. The process includes:

  • Secure endpoint for receiving notifications
  • Verification of notification authenticity
  • Encrypted storage of transaction records
  • Automatic processing and logging
  • Error handling and retry mechanisms

Your Rights and Control

  • Transparency: You can request information about what transaction data we store
  • Deletion: You can request deletion of transaction records (subject to legal requirements)
  • Access: You can access your purchase history through your Apple ID account
  • Control: You control all aspects of your Pearl content and medical data

Future Considerations

While Pearl is currently a one-time purchase, we may introduce additional features in the future. If we add subscription services or in-app purchases, App Store Server Notifications will help us manage those transactions securely and reliably.

Contact Information

If you have questions about App Store Server Notifications or your Pearl purchase:

  • Purchase Issues: Contact Apple Support through your App Store account
  • App Functionality: Contact our support team
  • Privacy Concerns: privacy@pearlapp.com
  • Technical Questions: support@pearlapp.com
Note: App Store Server Notifications are a standard part of App Store transactions and help ensure secure, reliable service for all users. They do not affect your privacy or the security of your medical data within Pearl.
