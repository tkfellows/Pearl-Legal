App Store Server Notifications

Last updated: January 2025

Note: Pearl is sold as a one-time purchase for $4.99. App Store Server Notifications are not required for one-time purchases, but we provide this information for transparency.

What are App Store Server Notifications?

App Store Server Notifications are webhooks that Apple sends to your server when certain events occur related to in-app purchases and subscriptions. These notifications help you track purchase status, subscription changes, and billing events.

Pearl's Purchase Model

  • One-time Purchase: Pearl is sold for $4.99 as a single purchase
  • No Subscriptions: We do not offer recurring subscriptions
  • No In-App Purchases: All features are included in the base purchase
  • No Server Required: Pearl operates entirely on your device

Why Not Required for Pearl?

  • One-time Purchase: No recurring billing to track
  • No Subscriptions: No subscription lifecycle events
  • Device-based: All functionality works offline
  • Simple Model: Purchase once, use forever
